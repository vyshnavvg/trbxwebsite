Hi, Greetings from TRBX



TRBX aka Team Roborex Started as the robotics team of Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering.

Our team started as a team time talk between students from different departments of SCTCE
The team was participating in robowar, roboscoccer, roborace, line follower and water rocket.
Then the team pivoted to podcasting, conducting workshops etc.
Team has faced a lot of hardships but never surrendered.
